Tributos List of Tlacozauhtitlan:

Every 6 months：

400 large white mantas

100 little pitchers of bees’honey

20 pans of a yellow varnish called tecoçahuitl

Annually:

1 yellow quaxolotl warrior costume and shield

Tributos List of Quiyauhteopan:

Every 6 months：

400 large white mantas

100 little jars of bees’ honey

80 little copper axes

40 large copper bells

Annually:

1 blue cuextecatl warrior costume and shield

1 little pan of small turquoise stones

Tributos List of Yoaltepec:

Every 6 months：

400 large white mantas

100 little jars of bees’ honey

Annually:

1 yellow warrior costume with quetzalpatzactli device, and shield

40 gold tiles

10 masks of turquoise-blue stones

1 large packet of turquoise stones
